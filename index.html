About Us

We Provide Innovative IoT Solutions for Breeding Material Preservation

We specialize in monitoring and preserving breeding materials across various species using innovative IoT-based solutions. Our technology ensures optimal storage conditions, safeguarding the integrity and viability of these essential resources. By precisely controlling factors such as temperature and humidity, we support agricultural and research communities in their breeding programs.

To be the global leader in breeding material preservation, advancing agricultural innovation and biodiversity through cutting-edge IoT technologies.

Our mission is to provide reliable, user-friendly, and scalable IoT solutions that empower our clients to maintain optimal storage conditions for breeding materials. We aim to enhance the quality and success rates of breeding efforts, supporting the agricultural and research sectors in securing the genetic diversity necessary for a sustainable future.

Services

We have devised a solution using IoT to address the challenges in storing breeding materials. Our solution encompasses:

Real-time Storage Monitoring

key parameters such as temperature, humidity, and light levels from the storage environment can be accessed in real-time on various devices like phones and tablets. This ensures the optimal preservation of your breeding materials and allows for immediate response to any changes in the storage conditions.

Proactive Alerts

Our system is designed to provide timely notifications of any deviations from the optimal storage conditions. These alerts are sent directly to your chosen device, such as a phone or tablet, allowing for immediate awareness and proactive corrective actions. This feature ensures that any potential issues are addressed promptly, minimizing the risk to your breeding materials and reducing the likelihood of significant losses.

Data-driven Decisions

Our system empowers you to make data-driven decisions by providing comprehensive analytics and insights. The real-time data collected from the storage environment is processed and presented in an easy-to-understand format on your chosen device. This allows you to gain a deeper understanding of the storage conditions and make informed decisions for optimal preservation of your breeding materials.
